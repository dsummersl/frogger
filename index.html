Look Before Your Cross!

Chapel Hill has the reputation of being a very pedestrian friendly town, yet its major roads are pretty intimidating! This game models the lanes and 2015 traffic counts of Chapel Hill's pedestrian-friendly streets.

Help our five school kids cross Chapel Hill's Streets as they wander the city.

Instructions:

  • Use arrow keys to control the player.
  • All five players need to cross the road to continue their journey.
  • Refresh the page to have a new start.
